Validity Gap (VG)

VG(s) = 1 − L(s)/N(s). Measures the proportion of papers that never evaluate whether students actually learned. VG = 1.000 means zero evidence of learning benefit across the entire sector.

Deployment Readiness Index (DRI)

DRI(s) = mean d(p). Scores: offline=0.00, crowdworker/LLM-sim=0.25, small user study=0.50, large user study=0.75, production=1.00. Boundaries follow clinical-trial phase criteria.

Multilingual Equity Index (MEI)

MEI(s) = mean m(p). Scores: English only=0.0, high-resource non-English=0.3, mid-resource primary=0.6, low-resource primary=0.8, South Asian primary=1.0. The ceiling is normative — South Asian languages serve 600M+ school-age learners yet have no dedicated educational NLP system in the 2022–2025 corpus.

Reference

Zahid et al. (2025). "A quantitative gap audit of LLM-era NLP for education: Validity, deployment, and multilingual equity." Field-wide results: VG=0.927, DRI=0.126, MEI=0.126. DRI correlates with learning-outcome evaluation at ρ=0.425, p<0.001.
